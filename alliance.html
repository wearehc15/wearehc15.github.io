Our Alliance

About HC15 Alliance

The HC15 is a European-based alliance of 13 diverse and accomplished women entrepreneurs who came together with the shared goals of collaboration, empowerment and support.

This alliance aims to provide a comprehensive and seamless support system for entrepreneurs on their entrepreneurial journey. By pooling our individual expertise, resources, and specialised offerings, the alliance becomes a one-stop hub, delivering a holistic range of solutions tailored to address every aspect of an entrepreneur's needs, thus fostering alignment and accelerated growth.
