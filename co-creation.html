CoCreation

What's the Co-Creation Program

The HC15 Co-Creation program is a full-service business support experience for women entrepreneurs where we will address more than just “The Business”.

Our program consists of three facilitated sessions where we discover the core of your motivation, business and means to implement with Sinek's Golden Circle model as the basis. We then validate your idea and/or services, in order to provide you with a doable plan tailored upon to you and your business needs.

Three sessions

Identifying the WHY

  • Motivations and Vision
  • Passions
  • Current obstructions
  • What you bring to the table
  • Your expertise

Finding the HOW

  • How to get there
  • Strategy
  • What you want to do
  • Conceptualization

Making the WHAT

  • Let's make THE plan
  • Delivery
  • Business functions
  • Measurables

Each stage is led by experienced entrepreneurs. The collaborative process is a unique flow of interchanging experts that cover a range of areas. Co-Creation sessions are set up in stages depending on where the individual is in their business, this will depend on the application filled out by the individuals.
